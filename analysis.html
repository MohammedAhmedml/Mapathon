Satellite-Based LULC & NDVI Analysis

Aligarh District | 2000 – 2025 Change Detection

LULC & NDVI Interpretation

The spatial analysis using Landsat imagery reveals significant urban expansion within the central Aligarh region between 2000 and 2025. Vegetation cover has declined near expanding residential zones, while water bodies remain relatively stable.

NDVI (Normalized Difference Vegetation Index) analysis indicates moderate vegetation health (0.4–0.6 range), with degraded patches near urban clusters.

This geospatial assessment highlights anthropogenic pressure and provides data-driven insights for sustainable land management.
